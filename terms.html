1.4 Care Instructions: Clients must provide clear, written care instructions for their pets. GentlePaws will follow these instructions to the best of our ability but may need to exercise professional judgment in unexpected situations.

2. Booking & Cancellation Policy

2.1 Booking Confirmation: All service bookings must be confirmed in writing (email or text) at least 48 hours in advance. Last-minute bookings may be accommodated subject to caregiver availability and may incur rush fees.

2.2 Cancellation by Client: Clients may cancel or reschedule services with at least 24 hours notice without penalty. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before scheduled service will be charged 50% of the service fee. Same-day cancellations will be charged 100% of the service fee.

2.3 Cancellation by GentlePaws: In rare circumstances where we must cancel service (caregiver illness, emergency, etc.), we will make every effort to provide a substitute caregiver or give as much advance notice as possible. No fees will be charged for services canceled by GentlePaws.

2.4 Holiday Bookings: Holiday bookings (Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year's, etc.) require at least 2 weeks advance notice and may include holiday service fees. Holiday cancellations require 48 hours notice.

3. Pet Health & Behavior Requirements

3.1 Vaccination Requirements: All pets must be current on required vaccinations appropriate to their species and age (rabies, distemper, bordetella for dogs in social settings, FVRCP for cats, etc.). Proof of vaccination may be requested.

3.2 Flea and Tick Prevention: Pets must be on current flea and tick prevention programs. GentlePaws reserves the right to refuse service if evidence of infestation is found.

3.3 Illness Disclosure: Clients must inform GentlePaws immediately if their pet shows signs of contagious illness (kennel cough, intestinal parasites, etc.). Service may be postponed until the pet is cleared by a veterinarian.

3.4 Behavior Assessment: GentlePaws reserves the right to refuse or discontinue service for any pet that displays aggressive behavior toward people or other animals that poses a safety risk. A behavioral assessment may be required for pets with a history of aggression.

3.5 Special Needs: Pets with medical conditions, mobility issues, anxiety, or other special needs must be disclosed during booking. Additional fees may apply for specialized care requirements.
