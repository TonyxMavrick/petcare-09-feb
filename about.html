About GentlePaws

We're a team of dedicated pet lovers providing safe, professional, and compassionate care for dogs and cats across New York City. Your pet's happiness and wellbeing are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Story: Born from Love & Need

GentlePaws was founded in 2018 by Sarah Mitchell, a lifelong animal lover who experienced firsthand the anxiety and uncertainty of finding trustworthy pet care. After struggling to find reliable, compassionate caregivers for her rescue dog Max during a family emergency, Sarah realized there was a real need for professional pet care that prioritized safety, communication, and genuine love for animals.

What started as Sarah caring for neighbors' pets has grown into a trusted team of experienced, background-checked pet care professionals serving families throughout New York City. We've cared for over 1,000 pets, built lasting relationships with hundreds of families, and maintained our founding promise: to treat every pet like our own.

Today, GentlePaws is known for our reliability, warm communication style, and unwavering commitment to pet safety and happiness. We're not just a pet care service — we're partners in your pet's health, happiness, and quality of life.

Our Mission

To provide pet parents with complete peace of mind through safe, reliable, and loving pet care services. We exist to ensure every dog and cat in our care receives the attention, exercise, comfort, and affection they deserve — while keeping their families informed and connected every step of the way.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and beloved pet care service in New York, known for our unwavering commitment to animal welfare, professional excellence, and genuine relationships with the families we serve. We envision a community where every pet parent has access to care they can trust completely.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make and every interaction we have with your beloved pets.

Genuine Love for Animals

Every team member is a true pet lover who treats your furry family members with the same care and affection they give their own pets. We're not just doing a job — we're living our passion.

Safety First, Always

From background-checked caregivers to emergency preparedness protocols, we never compromise on safety. Your pet's wellbeing is our top responsibility in every interaction.

Clear Communication

We believe pet parents deserve peace of mind through regular updates, photos, and open dialogue about their pet's care, routines, and any concerns that arise.

Professional Excellence

Our team receives ongoing training in pet behavior, first aid, and best care practices. We maintain the highest professional standards while keeping our approach warm and personal.
