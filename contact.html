Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book pet care services?

Simply fill out the contact form above, call us, or send an email. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your pet's needs, answer questions, and create a customized care plan. Once you're ready, we'll set up your first visit!

Do you offer meet-and-greet appointments?

Yes! We encourage all new clients to schedule a complimentary meet-and-greet so you can meet the caregiver, ask questions, and ensure your pet feels comfortable. This helps us understand your pet's personality and your home routine.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We understand plans change! We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or schedule changes. Please see our Terms & Conditions for our full cancellation policy.

Are your caregivers insured and bonded?

Yes, all GentlePaws caregivers are fully bonded and insured for your complete peace of mind. We also carry liability insurance to protect you, your pet, and your home.

Will I get updates about my pet?
