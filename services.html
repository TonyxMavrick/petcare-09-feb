Professional Pet Care Services

Comprehensive, personalized care for dogs and cats of all ages, breeds, and personalities. Every service is customized to your pet's unique needs and your schedule.

Pet Sitting

Comfort and care in the safety of their own home

Our professional pet sitting services provide your furry family members with personalized attention, feeding, playtime, and companionship while you're away. We maintain your pet's regular routine in the comfort and security of their own environment, reducing stress and ensuring they feel loved and safe.

What's Included

  • Scheduled daily visits (1, 2, or 3 times per day)
  • Feeding and fresh water according to your schedule
  • Playtime, cuddles, and companionship
  • Litter box cleaning or outdoor bathroom breaks
  • Medication administration if needed
  • Mail and package collection
  • Plant watering and basic home security checks
  • Daily photo and text updates

Who It's For

Perfect for cats, small dogs, senior pets, or any animal who feels most comfortable at home. Ideal for working professionals, weekend getaways, or extended travel.

Customization Options

Every pet sitting plan is customized to your pet's unique needs, personality, and routine. We'll discuss feeding schedules, favorite toys, behavioral quirks, and any special requirements during your initial consultation.

Dog Walking

Exercise, exploration, and happy tails

Regular exercise is essential for your dog's physical health and mental wellbeing. Our experienced dog walkers provide safe, energizing walks tailored to your dog's age, breed, and energy level. Whether your pup needs a quick potty break or an hour-long adventure, we ensure they get the outdoor time they need to stay healthy, happy, and well-behaved.

What's Included

  • Individual or small group walks (your choice)
  • 15, 30, or 60-minute walk durations
  • Flexible daily, weekly, or on-demand scheduling
  • Poop bags and cleanup included
  • Fresh water provided during longer walks
  • GPS tracking available for peace of mind
  • Post-walk report with photos
  • Paw wiping and towel-off in bad weather

Who It's For

Ideal for busy professionals, high-energy breeds, puppies learning leash skills, or any dog who needs regular exercise and stimulation. Great for dogs of all ages and abilities.

Overnight Pet Care

24/7 supervision and complete peace of mind

When you need to be away overnight, our caregivers stay in your home to provide round-the-clock attention and security for your pets. We maintain their normal routines, offer companionship throughout the evening, and ensure someone is always there in case of emergency. Sleep soundly knowing your pets are safe, comfortable, and never alone.

What's Included

  • Full overnight stay (typically 8pm-8am or customized)
  • Evening and morning feeding, walks, and care
  • Bedtime routines and companionship
  • Overnight supervision and security
  • Multiple bathroom breaks for dogs
  • Medication administration as needed
  • Home security presence
  • Morning and evening photo updates

Who It's For

Perfect for anxious pets who don't do well alone, senior animals needing overnight monitoring, or pet parents who travel frequently and want comprehensive care and home security.

Puppy & Kitten Care

Patient, nurturing attention for your youngest pets

Young pets need extra patience, supervision, and care during their crucial early months. Our puppy and kitten care services provide gentle handling, socialization support, consistent routines, and the attentive monitoring young animals require as they grow and learn. We help set them up for success with positive experiences and loving attention.

What's Included

  • Frequent potty break visits for puppies
  • Consistent feeding schedules
  • Gentle play and early socialization
  • Basic training reinforcement
  • Accident cleanup and patience
  • Age-appropriate exercise
  • Close monitoring for health and behavior
  • Detailed progress reports

Who It's For

Essential for new puppy or kitten parents who work during the day, need help with house training, or want professional support during the demanding early months of pet parenthood.

Senior Pet Care

Gentle, compassionate attention for aging companions

Senior pets deserve extra comfort, patience, and specialized care as they age. Our senior pet care services are designed specifically for older animals who may have mobility challenges, medical needs, or simply need a gentler pace. We provide the tender, understanding attention that helps your aging pet maintain quality of life and dignity.

What's Included

  • Gentle assistance with mobility and stairs
  • Frequent bathroom breaks and patience
  • Medication administration and health monitoring
  • Comfortable, low-stress interactions
  • Arthritis-friendly exercise and movement
  • Special diet feeding and hydration support
  • Extra time for slower-paced activities
  • Detailed health observations and updates

Who It's For

Perfect for pet parents with aging dogs or cats who need extra support, have medical conditions, or require careful monitoring and gentle handling throughout the day.

Pet Feeding & Medication Support

Reliable care for pets with dietary and medical needs

Pets with special diets, medication schedules, or health conditions need consistent, reliable care. Our feeding and medication support services ensure your pet receives the right food, supplements, and medicines at precisely the right times. We follow your veterinarian's instructions exactly and keep detailed records for your peace of mind.

What's Included

  • Scheduled feeding according to your vet's plan
  • Oral medication administration
  • Insulin injections and glucose monitoring (if trained)
  • Special diet preparation and portion control
  • Supplement administration
  • Detailed care logs and medication tracking
  • Health observations and behavior monitoring
  • Communication with your veterinarian if needed

Who It's For

Essential for pets with diabetes, chronic conditions, post-surgery recovery, or any animal requiring regular medication or specialized feeding schedules.
